# E

- 3. Problem solving/Decision making

Problem solving—Identifies problems, involves others in seek- ing solutions, conducts appropriate analyses, searches for the best solutions, responds quickly to new challenges. Decision making—Makes clear, consistent, transparent decisions; acts with integrity in all decision making; distinguishes relevant from irrelevant information and makes timely decisions.
