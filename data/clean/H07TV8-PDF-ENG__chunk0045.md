# FR

Felix Rüdiger is head content & research of the St. Gallen Symposium, a global platform for cross-generational dialogue at the University of St. Gallen. He co-leads the symposium’s joint initiative with the Club of Rome for “A New Generational Contract,” which fosters long-term, sustainable strategizing in business and public policy.
