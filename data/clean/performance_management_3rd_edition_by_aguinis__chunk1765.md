## TABLE 10.3 Recommendations for Making Rewards Work

Define and measure performance first, and then allocate rewards. Use only rewards that are available. Make sure all employees are eligible. Make rewards visible.

Make rewards contingent. Make rewards timely.

Make rewards reversible. Use nonfinancial rewards.
