## TABLE 10.1 Reasons Why Contingent Pay Plans Fail

A poor performance management system is in place. There is the folly of rewarding A while hoping for B. Rewards are not significant. Managers are not accountable. There exists an extrinsic motivation at the expense of intrinsic motivation. Rewards for executives are disproportionately large compared to rewards for everyone else.
