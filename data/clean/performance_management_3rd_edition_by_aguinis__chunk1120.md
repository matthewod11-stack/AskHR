Part II—Goals from Last Year or Last Evaluation Period Rate the progress made on each of the goals established at the beginning of the period. Also include any new goals established during the evaluation period and note any modifications to the original goals.

Part III—Goals for This Coming Year or Evaluation Period Enter the performance goals for the next period to be evaluated. Individual goals and objectives should align with those of the department and the campus.

Part IV—Professional Development Plan Enter any actions that will be taken by the employee or manager/supervisor to support the goals indicated in Part III above, or specific job success factors in Part I. The plan may include career growth, job mastery, or actions to correct performance.
