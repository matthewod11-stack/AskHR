# Situational Engineering

This approach would appear quite promising, as there is a growing body of evidence that conditions at work affect employee PWB (Wright & Cropanzano, 2000). For instance, research has shown that something as basic as providing tangible social support on the job can minimize the negative impact of a stressful work environment (Kohn & Schooler, 1982).
