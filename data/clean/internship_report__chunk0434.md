## different business concerns of this group.

In other word, we can say, this group plays a vital role for employment creation. As a result,

strong HR policy is crucial for smooth people management and development.

This group has about 56 year developed HR policy which most comply with the Bangladesh

labor act 2006. Moreover, this organization has many very experienced people. However, few IT

based HR tools are required for modernized HR practices. On the other hand, continuous on-job

and external training can prepare remarkable human resource for the organization and those

people can achieve the organizational goal.
